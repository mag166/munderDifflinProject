About Us

We are a full-service Paper Company based in Scranton, PA. We are here for all your business paper needs. We sell a variety of paper products, printers and general office supplies. Paper is a thin material produced by pressing together moist fibres of cellulose pulp derived from wood, rags or grasses, and drying them into flexible sheets. It is a versatile material with many uses, including writing, printing, packaging, cleaning, and a number of industrial and construction processes.
